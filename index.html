Dental Treatment in Iran Dental treatment in Iran includes the best quality medical or cosmetic dentistry at the cost of up to one-fifth compared to other countries. With approximately 40,000 dentists, Iran is among the top 15 countries in terms of dentists' number. Most Iranian dentists have specialized medical boards from the best European and American universities. Meanwhile, the cost of having dental treatment in Iran compared to Western countries such as the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, Germany, and other countries in the region such as Turkey, India, and the UAE is between 50% and 70% cheaper.
